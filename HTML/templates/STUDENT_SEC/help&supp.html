Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by going to Settings → Security → Reset Password.

How do I contact support?

Use the Contact Support form in the Help & Support section, or email us at support@eduportal.com.

Where can I see my class schedule?

Your class schedule is available in the Dashboard → Class Search section.

How do I apply for hostel/transport facility?

Visit Services > Hostel/Transport, fill the application form, and submit it online..

How do I pay my fees online?

Open the Administration section, Fees Section, select the pending fees, and complete payment using UPI, Net Banking, or Debit/Credit card.
